Usage Rules

User requirement

The service is available to drivers aged 21 and old with driving experience category «B» from 2 years. For business class car rental you must be at least 25 years with at least 5 years of experience category «B».

Passing the car on

It is strictly forbidden to transfer control of the car to another driver - this is a serious violation of the rules of service and traffic. If something happens to the car, you will be obliged to pay all the costs that the company will incur. Insurance in this case is not valid.

Charging and Bonuses

Charge "Greencar" - easy! Charge cars only in places, which are provided for it. For successful charging we will score bonus points that you can spend during the trip.

Insurance and extended CASCO

All cars are insured. You do not have to pay anything if you are not responsible for the accident. Otherwise, the amount of payment depends on the damage, but not more than 3,000 rubles. Connect the extended CASCO and in any accident you will pay no more than 1,000 rubles.

Car inspection for damage

Sometimes the car may be damaged or missing any element, such as carpets. Before starting If you do not do so, you will be responsible for them. If you do not do so, you will be responsible for them.

Compliance with traffic and parking regulations

The driver is always liable for traffic violations and fines. We receive fines and immediately pay them. Then send the invoice to the driver for payment with commission depending on the amount of the fine.
